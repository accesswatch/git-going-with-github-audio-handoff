Let's finish with common issues and workarounds, because these practical frustrations matter.

[JAMIE]
First: VoiceOver skips some pull request descriptions.

[ALEX]
A good workaround is to switch the rotor to Headings or Links and navigate by structure instead of plain sequential swiping. If the description contains complex formatting, try moving by headings, then by links, and explore the content in smaller pieces. If necessary, reopen the item or use another navigation mode rather than assuming the content is absent.

[JAMIE]
Second: TalkBack does not announce the new notifications badge.

[ALEX]
In that case, don't rely only on the badge. Open the Notifications tab directly and review the list. Mobile badges can be inconsistent across apps and devices, so the reliable method is to check the tab itself.

[JAMIE]
Third: the keyboard covers the comment field.

[ALEX]
This is a common mobile problem. After activating the comment field, you may need to scroll slightly, dismiss and reopen the keyboard, rotate the device if your setup allows, or move focus carefully until the submit control comes back into view. On either platform, patience and incremental navigation help.

[JAMIE]

[ALEX]
This often means focus is still trapped near the keyboard or text field. Try navigating past the editing area deliberately. On iOS, rotor settings like Form Controls can help jump among interactive elements. On Android, move through controls methodically or adjust granularity if needed. [ALEX]
The practical steps are the usual ones: force close and reopen the app, make sure it's updated, and if the problem persists, sign out and sign back in through Profile and Settings. If you still cannot complete the task, switch to the web or desktop workflow so your work is not blocked.
