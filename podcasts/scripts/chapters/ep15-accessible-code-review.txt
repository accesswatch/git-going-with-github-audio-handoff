What makes an inline comment useful?

[ALEX]
Yes. Comments should be constructive, specific, and proportionate. Don’t leave “Looks good” on a serious accessibility issue. Don’t write a novel for a tiny nit unless the repo culture expects exhaustive review. Match the comment to the importance of the issue.

What makes a useful review comment?

[ALEX]

[JAMIE]
Blocking, non-blocking nit, and question?

[ALEX]

[ALEX]

[ALEX]
There isn’t a fixed number. Too many comments is when the volume stops helping. Ten precise comments on ten real issues may be excellent. Three vague comments may be worse. Group related feedback when possible, avoid repetitive phrasing, and make sure each comment adds distinct value.
